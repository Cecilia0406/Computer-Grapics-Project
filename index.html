Project A: Into the Star Wars

Instructions

  1. Use the "Run/Stop" button to pause or run the animation.
  2. Use the "Spin" buttons to increase or decrease the rate the Star spins.
  3. Input an angle into the submission box to change the angle of the Star and Fishbone.
  4. Click and drag to turn the 3D Tie Spaceship.
  5. Double-click and drag to move the 3D Tie Spaceship on the canvas.
  6. Use the "W", "A", "S", and "D" keys to move the Star on the canvas.
